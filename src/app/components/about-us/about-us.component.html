About Us

Hello everyone! I’m Christopher Black, a South African originally from Mossel Bay, and a passionate digital nomad who’s called Cape Town home for the past 8 years.

After traveling and working remotely, I discovered a love for connecting with others on the move and found my inspiration in being part of various communities in South East Asia and Cape Town. Cape Town is a paradise for digital nomads, but with so many people coming and going, it can be challenging to find a lasting community.

I enjoy meeting new people, hosting events, and uncovering the hidden gems around town. That’s why I’m creating a platform where digital nomads like us can truly connect, share stories, and swap travel tips to get the most out of life in Cape Town.

Whether you're exploring trails, checking out unique bars and restaurants, or looking for your next adventure, this platform is for everyone wanting to embrace the digital nomad lifestyle.
